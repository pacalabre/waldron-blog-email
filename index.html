Senior Living Leaders Take Responsible Approach to HQ Closure

When Emeritus Senior Living and Brookdale Senior Living announced an agreement to merge, creating a combined entity with more than 1,100 senior living communities in 47 states and a workforce of more than 80,000, a smooth integration immediately became the company’s top priority. Over 300 people’s jobs were impacted by the closure, representing multiple functions, levels, and locations.

Emeritus/Brookdale enlisted Waldron’s support in planning for the closure and supporting the career transition needs of all impacted employees in a way that aligned with organization culture and values.
